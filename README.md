# AI-Powered-Recruitment-Research-Assistant

1. To automate the collection of company and college information from publicly available web sources.
2. To generate structured business intelligence reports using AI.
3. To assist recruiters, HR consultants, and business development teams in identifying potential client companies.
4. To provide hiring insights, recruitment activity analysis, and company growth information.
5. To reduce manual research effort and improve lead generation efficiency.


# Summary :- 

1. The AI-Powered Recruitment Research Assistant is an intelligent information extraction system that combines web search capabilities with Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate detailed company and college profiles. 
2. The application collects data from online sources using Tavily Search and processes the information using Mistral AI to create structured reports. 
3. For companies, it provides business intelligence such as GST details, employee strength, revenue information, hiring activity, leadership details, and recruitment insights. 
4. For colleges, it gathers institutional information including contact details, courses, departments, and student statistics. 
5. The system helps recruiters, staffing agencies, and business development professionals identify potential clients and hiring opportunities efficiently.


# Key Features :- 

Company Intelligence Module - 

1. Company overview generation
2. Industry and sub-industry identification
3. Registered office and headquarters details
4. GSTIN, CIN, and company registration information
5. Employee strength estimation
6. Revenue and business growth insights
7. Directors and leadership information
8. Official website and LinkedIn extraction

Recruitment Intelligence Module - 

1. Current hiring status detection
2. Active job openings identification
3. Automated web research

College Information Module - 

1. College profile generation
2. Student and faculty information
3. Course and department extraction
4. Contact and address details collection
5. Automated web research
6. Multi-source information aggregation
7. Structured report generation
8. Missing information handling using "Not Found"


# Result :-

1. Successfully automates company and college data collection from web sources.
2. Generates structured business intelligence reports within seconds.
3. Provides recruitment-related insights useful for staffing and HR agencies.
4. Reduces manual research time significantly.
5. Improves lead identification and prospect analysis for recruitment services.
6. Produces organized and easy-to-read reports from unstructured web data.


# Conclusion :- 

The AI-Powered Recruitment Research Assistant demonstrates how Large Language Models and web search technologies can be integrated to automate business research and recruitment intelligence gathering. The system efficiently extracts and organizes company and college information, helping recruiters, HR consultants, and business development teams make informed decisions. By reducing manual effort and delivering structured insights, the solution enhances productivity and supports data-driven recruitment and lead generation processes.


# Technology Used :-

1. Programming Language - Python
2. AI & ML - Mistral Large Language Model (LLM)
3. Frameworks & Libraries - LangChain , LangChain MistralAI , Python Dotenv
4. Search & Data Collection - Tavily Search API
5. Ui - Streamlit
